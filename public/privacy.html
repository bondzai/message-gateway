Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account, we collect:

  • Your TikTok user profile (display name, username, avatar)
  • Direct messages sent and received through TikTok
  • Authentication tokens to maintain your connection

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Display and manage your TikTok conversations
  • Send replies on your behalf when you use the Service
  • Maintain your account connection

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Authentication tokens are encrypted. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only while your account is connected. When you disconnect your TikTok account, your authentication tokens are immediately deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with TikTok's API. Your use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's Terms of Service and TikTok's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

You may disconnect your TikTok account and delete your data at any time through the Service dashboard.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through the Service dashboard.
